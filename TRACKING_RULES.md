Primary Muscle Philosophy
-------------------------
Primary muscles represent the muscle(s) intentionally trained by the exercise,
not every muscle contributing to the movement.

Volume Philosophy
-----------------
Only dynamic hypertrophy exercises contribute to muscle volume.

Ignored Exercises
-----------------
- Farmer Walk
- Pushup
- Hanging Knee Raise
- Band Pushdown
- Banded Face Pull

Load Multipliers
----------------
If the logged weight represents one dumbbell or one side of a machine,
multiply by two when calculating volume.

Bodyweight
----------
Pull-ups, Chin-ups, Dips, Back Extensions, Decline Abs and Standing Calf Raises use
bodyweight plus external load.
